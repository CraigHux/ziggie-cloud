---

## Don't Mix These Up

❌ **WRONG:** "Ziggie is the game"
✅ **RIGHT:** "Meow Ping RTS is the game; Ziggie is the platform that develops it"

❌ **WRONG:** "Meow Ping RTS manages Ziggie"
✅ **RIGHT:** "Ziggie manages Meow Ping RTS"

❌ **WRONG:** "Use Meow Ping RTS to generate assets"
✅ **RIGHT:** "Use Ziggie's asset pipeline to generate assets for Meow Ping RTS"

## When Writing Documentation

### Use **Meow Ping RTS** for:
- Game mechanics
- Gameplay features
- Player progression
- Unit/character systems
- Combat, waves, balance
- Player-facing APIs
- Game design documents

### Use **Ziggie** for:
- AI agents
- Development workflows
- Asset generation
- Control Center
- Knowledge base
- Project management
- Development infrastructure

### Use **BOTH** for:
- How Ziggie develops Meow Ping RTS
- Integration points
- Architecture docs (when showing both systems)
- Explaining the full ecosystem

## Tone Guide

### Meow Ping RTS Tone
- Friendly and engaging
- Encouraging
- Playful but professional
- Player-focused
- Example: "Show them what you're made of!"

### Ziggie Tone
- Technical and precise
- Professional and confident
- Developer-focused
- Innovation-oriented
- Example: "Orchestrate your development workflow"

## Standard Phrases

### Introducing Them Separately

**Meow Ping RTS:**
> "Meow Ping RTS is a cat-themed real-time strategy game where players lead cat armies to victory."

**Ziggie:**
> "Ziggie is an AI-powered development platform that orchestrates 584 agents to efficiently create games like Meow Ping RTS."

### Introducing Them Together

> "Meow Ping RTS is developed and managed by Ziggie, our AI-powered development platform."

Or:

> "Ziggie demonstrates its power through Meow Ping RTS, a cat-themed real-time strategy game."

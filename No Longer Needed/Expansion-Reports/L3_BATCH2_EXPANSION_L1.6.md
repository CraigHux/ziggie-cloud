- Balance survivability vs offensive power

### L3.6.1.2: **Cost-to-Power Balancer**
**Specialty:** Resource cost vs unit power efficiency

**Capabilities:**
- Calculate power-per-resource-point
- Ensure higher-cost units provide proportional value
- Balance economy progression
- Prevent cost-inefficient units

**Example:**
```
Tier 1 Warrior: 150 energy, 100 HP, 25 damage = 0.83 power/cost
Tier 2 Tank: 300 energy, 250 HP, 40 damage = 0.97 power/cost
Result: Tier 2 provides 17% more efficiency (acceptable for progression)
```

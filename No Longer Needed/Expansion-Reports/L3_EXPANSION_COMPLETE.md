# L3 MICRO-AGENT ARCHITECTURE EXPANSION
## Version 3.0 - From 729 to 1,728 L3 Agents

**Date:** 2025-11-08
**Expansion:** +999 New L3 Micro-Agents
**New Structure:** 12 L1 × 12 L2 × 12 L3 = 1,728 Total L3 Agents

---

## EXPANSION SUMMARY

This document details the complete expansion of the L3 Micro-Agent architecture from 729 to 1,728 agents.

**Expansion Breakdown:**
- **Phase 1:** Add L3.10, L3.11, L3.12 to existing 81 L2 agents = 243 new L3s
- **Phase 2:** Add L2.10, L2.11, L2.12 to existing 9 L1 agents (12 L3s each) = 324 new L3s
- **Phase 3:** Add L1.10, L1.11, L1.12 (12 L2s × 12 L3s each) = 432 new L3s
- **TOTAL:** 999 new L3 agents

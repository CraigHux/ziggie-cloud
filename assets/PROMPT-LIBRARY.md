## CAT DESIGN PRINCIPLES (From Lore Bible)

1. **Feline anatomy preserved** - Ears, whiskers, tails are NEVER hidden
2. **Battle-scarred** - Missing ear tips, cybernetic eye replacements, fur patterns broken by scars
3. **Augmentation visible** - Exposed circuitry, glowing implants, mechanical limbs
4. **Equipment is salvaged** - Nothing looks factory-new
5. **Eyes glow with data-light** - Cyan, amber, or red depending on role

### Eye Color by Role
| Role | Eye Glow Color |
|------|----------------|
| Warriors | Amber/orange |
| Mages (Tech-Casters) | Cyan/blue |
| Engineers | Green/yellow |
| Scouts | Purple/violet |
| Commanders | White/gold |
